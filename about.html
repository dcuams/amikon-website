About Us

Amikon is an Anime and Manga convention ran "yearly" by the Anime & Manga Society in DCU.

The convention entails 2 days of events, Saturday and Sunday, where many activities are held in the U Building of Dublin City University.

After a long break due to COVID-19, Amikon is back on May 20th and 21st for its second year, 2023.
